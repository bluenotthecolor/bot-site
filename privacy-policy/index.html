Privacy Policy for Lana Bot

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

1. Data Collection

Lana Bot collects data only when necessary to provide its requested services. This data may include:

  • Server Data — Server IDs, channel IDs, and role IDs to allow the bot to function within your specific server environment.
  • User Data — Discord User IDs for tracking settings, permissions, or activity within the bot's features (like moderation or custom utilities).
  • Logs — Temporary logs of command usage and event triggers to ensure the bot operates correctly and to troubleshoot technical issues.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Feature Provisioning — To execute the commands requested by you or your server administrators.
  • Service Improvement — To analyze how the bot is used so we can improve performance and reliability.
  • Security — To prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Protection

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. All data is handled with the objective of providing a secure experience. We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as it is necessary to provide the services offered by Lana Bot. If a server removes the bot, we make reasonable efforts to clear that server's specific data from our active databases.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the removal of your server's data. To do so, please contact the development team through our support server. You can also remove Lana Bot from your server at any time by kicking or banning the bot from your Discord server.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to the development team on our support server.
